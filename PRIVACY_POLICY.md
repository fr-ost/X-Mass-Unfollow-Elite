# Privacy Policy

X Mass Unfollow Elite

Developed by Shahriar Ahmed

## Data collection
This extension does not collect, store, transmit, sell, or share personal information.

## Local storage
The extension uses Chrome storage only to save user-selected settings, such as delay values, max session limit, cooldown settings, and behavior toggles.

## Third-party services
This extension does not use analytics, advertising, tracking scripts, or external data collection services.

## Website access
The extension runs only on x.com and twitter.com pages to perform its stated single purpose: helping the user manage their own following list.
